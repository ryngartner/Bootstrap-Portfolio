The only thing worse than yellow snow is green snow. We want to use a lot pressure while using no pressure at all. We're not trying to teach you a thing to copy. We're just here to teach you a technique, then let you loose into the world. Happy painting, God bless. It all happens automatically. Exercising the imagination, experimenting with talents, being creative; these things, to me, are truly the windows to your soul.

I thought today we would make a happy little stream that's just running through the woods here. I want everbody to be happy. That's what it's all about. Just take out whatever you don't want. It'll change your entire perspective. This piece of canvas is your world. When things happen - enjoy them. They're little gifts.
